Transfer options

Private transfer

(5,000 THB per way, 6 pers. Maximum)
  • Taxi (sedan or minivan) from airport or hotel in Phuket / Krabi
  • Private Longtail Boat
  • Local taxi from Koh Yao Noi pier to the hotel
    (if direct arrival on the hotel beach is not possible)

Simple Transfer

(1,200 THB one way per adult and 500 THB per child)
  • Taxi (sedan or minivan) from airport or hotel in Phuket / Krabi
  • Public speedboat or longtail boat according to schedule.
  • Local taxi from Koh Yao Noi pier to the hotel
