About
Koh Yao Noi

Laguna Villas is a modern beachside resort located on Koh Yao Noi. In the center of Ao Phang-Nga National Park and most attractive island, Yao Noi is the best starting point to explore the famous marine park and its classic limestone rock formations. Facing this breathtaking scenery, Laguna Villas is ideally situated so you can access by a walking distance many other highlights of Koh Yao Noi. Our Deluxe Villas offer the utmost comfort often sought in a tropical environment so you can experience exhilarating activities and relax in complete serenity.
